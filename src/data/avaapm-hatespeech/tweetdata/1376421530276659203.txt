29 Mar 1918, Brig-Gen Harry Fulton CMG DSO, Commander #NewZealand Rifle Brigade, died of wounds, aged 48. Buried Doullens Military Cemetery. Born India. Family emigrated to NZ. 1892, enlisted in British Army. Fought in 2nd Boer War &amp; in #WW1 in German Samoa, N Africa &amp; Flanders. https://t.co/xHbDxsN6mD
